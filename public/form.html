General Rules :

1. A team is allowed to have maximum 4 members in Business Modules , 3 members in IPL Auction and 2 members in Business Quiz.

2. After registering successfully for the Business Modules, the team is free to participate in Bonfire Chat,Intern Fair and Gala Dinner and along with this Kits and T-shirts will be provided.

3. Business Modules pass is for Pitching Competition, Board Room and Business Model Presentation.

4. Business Quiz pass and IPL Auction pass is for those who wish to participate in Business Quiz or IPL Auction only.

5. The registration fee for the Stock Market Workshop is Rs.100 per head which covers the course fee. Along with this Demat Account will be provided to all the participants of workshop to start your trading venture.

6. The accommodation charge is Rs.200 per head which includes fooding and stay.

7. Until the Payment is not completed, your registration is not valid.
